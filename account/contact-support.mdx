## Email is the fastest channel

[hi@spotlightvendor.com](mailto:hi@spotlightvendor.com)

We read every email. For most requests, expect a reply within **one business day**. For urgent issues affecting your facility's day-to-day operations, put **URGENT** in the subject line — we triage those first.

## What to include

To help us help you faster:

- **Your account email** so we can pull up your account
- **Your facility or vendor company name**
- **Specific URLs** if it's a problem with a page (paste the link from your browser address bar)
- **Screenshots** of any error messages
- **What you were trying to do** when the issue happened

## Common issues we can resolve same-day

- Reset password / locked out of account
- Vendor invitation didn't arrive
- Compliance document review status questions
- Billing or invoice questions
- Adding a new facility to your organization
- Changing the primary contact on an account

## What's NOT support

For sales inquiries, partnerships, demos, or press: same email — [hi@spotlightvendor.com](mailto:hi@spotlightvendor.com) — we'll route it appropriately.
