Two sides of the platform pay for what matters most to them.

- **Facilities and Property Managers** pay a **per-facility license** for governance and audit infrastructure — vendor compliance tracking, RFP and project management, in-app chat, role-based access, and access to network purchasing power.
- **Vendors** pay a subscription for reach — to be discoverable to facilities, get RFP matches, and bid on competitive work.

## Pricing at a glance

| Side | Price | Billing |
|---|---|---|
| **Facility license** | **$249 / month per facility** | Billed annually |
| **Vendor — Free** | $0 | — |
| **Vendor — Pro** | $39 / month or $429 / year | Monthly or annual |

Each facility = one license. A property management company managing 10 facilities holds 10 facility licenses under one organization.

## Quick answers

**Where do I see my plan?** In your portal, click your name (top right) → Billing.

**Can I cancel anytime?** Vendors on monthly Pro can cancel anytime. Facility licenses and annual vendor subscriptions run for the full term.

**What happens if a license isn't renewed?** The organization is suspended (read-only) until renewal — your data stays intact, but you can't post new RFPs or invite vendors.

**Do you offer custom enterprise pricing?** Yes — for property management companies with 25+ facilities, email [hi@spotlightvendor.com](mailto:hi@spotlightvendor.com) and we'll tailor a contract.
