Two sides of the platform pay for what matters most to them.

- **Vendors** pay a subscription for reach — to be discoverable to facilities, get RFP matches, and bid on competitive work.
- **Facilities** pay annual per-facility contracts for governance and audit infrastructure — vendor compliance tracking, RFP management, and a clean documented paper trail.

## Quick answers

**Where do I see my plan?** In your portal, click your name (top right) → Billing.

**Can I cancel anytime?** Vendors on monthly Pro can cancel anytime. Annual subscriptions and facility contracts run for the full term.

**Do you offer custom enterprise pricing?** Yes — for multi-site operators (10+ facilities), email [hi@spotlightvendor.com](mailto:hi@spotlightvendor.com) and we'll tailor a contract.
