## How facility licensing works

SpotlightVendor charges a license **per facility**. Each license is a year-long term, billed annually.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Pricing** | Per facility, billed annually. [Request a quote →](mailto:hi@spotlightvendor.com?subject=Facility%20license%20quote) |
| **Term** | 12 months from your start date |
| **Renewal** | Auto-renews annually unless cancelled |
| **Add a facility mid-term** | Pro-rated for the remainder of your term, then renews on your standard cycle |

A property management company managing 10 facilities holds 10 facility licenses under one organization. That single organization umbrella gives you portfolio-wide rollups and cross-facility compliance — see [For Property Managers](/property-managers).

## What's included with each license

- Unlimited vendor invitations and onboarding for that facility
- Compliance document tracking (W-9, COI, Workers' Comp + any facility-specific docs you require)
- Automatic 30-day expiration alerts
- Unlimited RFPs, proposals, and project tracking
- In-app chat with vendors
- Network access — including discovery of pre-vetted vendors and access to negotiated network pricing
- Role-based team access (organization admins, facility managers)
- Audit-ready reports

## Adding facilities mid-term

If you add a facility 4 months into a 12-month term, that facility is licensed for the remaining 8 months at a pro-rated rate. At the next annual renewal, all your facility licenses renew together on a standard cycle.

## Suspension and renewal

If a license isn't renewed by its end date, the organization moves into a suspended state. Your data is preserved, but you can't post new RFPs, invite new vendors, or create new compliance requests until the license is renewed. Existing data and history stay intact.
