## When to upgrade to Pro

**Free is fine if:**
- You're already invited to bid by facilities you have an established relationship with
- You don't need to actively grow your customer base on the platform
- You're testing whether SpotlightVendor is right for you before committing

**Pro is worth it when:**
- You want to grow — Pro is the path to discovery and matched RFPs you wouldn't otherwise see
- You're investing in your platform presence and want the credibility of priority placement and the Pro badge
- You want to track your own performance metrics over time
- You typically win at least one Pro-tier RFP per quarter — it pays for itself

## How to upgrade

In your vendor portal: Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription. Stripe handles the checkout and you can switch between monthly and annual at any renewal.

## Cancel anytime

Monthly Pro subscribers can cancel anytime — your Pro features stay active until the end of the current billing cycle. Annual subscribers can cancel at the end of the term (or contact support for early termination).
