## What SpotlightVendor does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Central, audit-ready compliance" icon="shield-check" href="/compliance">
    Every vendor's W-9, COI, and Workers' Comp tracked, expiration alerts 30 days out, and a clean documented trail for surveys and audits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vetting at the org or facility level" icon="layers" href="/compliance/levels-explained">
    Approve a vendor once for your whole organization, or require facility-specific documents on top. Flexible based on how strict each facility needs to be.
  </Card>
  <Card title="RFPs, bids, and project tracking" icon="file-pen" href="/facilities/post-an-rfp">
    Post a request, get bids back, award the work, and track the project end-to-end with all documents in one place.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Performance insights for vendors and facilities" icon="chart-line" href="/vendors">
    Vendors see their win rate, completion rate, and active proposals. Facilities see their own RFP activity and vendor performance. Each side sees what's relevant to them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Portfolio-wide rollup for property managers" icon="chart-pie" href="/property-managers">
    If you manage multiple facilities, the org dashboard sums activity across every facility under you — one view of how the whole portfolio is performing.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
