If your facility's vendor list lives in a spreadsheet, three people's email folders, and a binder somewhere in the administrator's office — you're not alone. Most senior living operators run their vendor network the same way, and most of them get burned the same way: a COI expires unnoticed, a surveyor asks for a document nobody can find, or a contractor on the property turns out not to be the contractor on the contract.

Spotlight Vendor is built to fix that. It's the operating system for your facility's vendor network — a single, organized, current source of truth for every vendor you work with, every document they've provided, and every project in motion.

## What it does

Four things, in plain language:

**Curate your vendor network.** Each facility builds its own list of approved, vetted vendors. Vendors create profiles and upload their own compliance documents — W-9s, certificates of insurance, workers' compensation, and anything else you require. Our team reviews each vendor's documents before they're verified.

**Keep credentials current — automatically.** Documents expire. Insurance lapses. Licenses renew on different cycles. Spotlight Vendor tracks every expiration date and emails vendors 30 days out to renew. If a vendor lets a critical document expire, their account drops to read-only — they can't bid on new work or accept invitations until they're current. Your network self-cleans.

**Run RFPs through your network.** When you need work done, post an RFP. Vendors in your network whose credentials are current and whose service categories match see it and can bid. You compare proposals, award the project, message the vendor, and review the work when it's done — all in one place.

**See performance insights scoped to your role.** Vendors see their own win rate, completion rate, and active proposals. Facilities see their own RFP activity and how their vendors are performing. If you're a multi-site operator or property management company, the org dashboard rolls up activity across every facility you oversee — so you can see the whole portfolio at a glance, then drill into any single building.

## Who it's for

- Senior living facility administrators who want vendor management to stop eating their week
- Multi-site operators and property management companies running vendor networks across multiple buildings, who need portfolio-wide visibility
- Compliance officers who own audit readiness and don't want to discover a problem during a survey
- Vendors serving senior care who want to maintain their credentials in one place and respond to bid requests faster
