If you manage senior living or healthcare facilities **on behalf of owners** — across multiple sites, often across multiple owners — SpotlightVendor gives you organization-level vendor management. One login, every facility's vendors, every facility's compliance, every facility's open RFPs.

This is different from the [self-managed Facilities](/facilities) workflow: that one's for operators running their own single site. Property Managers get cross-portfolio rollups, comparative spend visibility, and the ability to set vendor and compliance standards once and apply them everywhere.

## Why this matters for property managers

| Pain | What SpotlightVendor does |
|---|---|
| Different facilities tracking compliance in different spreadsheets | One dashboard, every facility, every vendor's documents in one place. |
| Chasing executive directors at each site for vendor info during transitions | Vendor history, contracts, and compliance docs travel with the facility — they don't leave when your ED does. |
| No visibility into which facilities are overpaying which vendors | Portfolio-wide spend visibility per category. Identify outliers, negotiate better. |
| Audit readiness across 20+ facilities at once | One report, every facility, every vendor's current docs and expiration dates. |

## Next step

Once you're set up, the most valuable first move is **adding all your facilities** under your organization. That gives you the cross-portfolio view that makes the rest worthwhile.
