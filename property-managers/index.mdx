## Why property managers choose SpotlightVendor

| Pain | What we do |
|---|---|
| Different facilities tracking compliance in different spreadsheets | One dashboard, every facility, every vendor's documents in one place. |
| Vendor history disappearing during executive director transitions | Vendor relationships, contracts, and compliance docs travel with the facility — they don't leave when your ED does. |
| No visibility into which facilities overpay which vendors | Portfolio-wide spend visibility per category. Identify outliers, negotiate better. |
| Audit readiness across 20+ facilities at once | One report, every facility, every vendor's current docs and expiration dates. |
| Limited leverage as individual facilities | Network purchasing power — negotiated vendor rates members access via the platform. |

## How role hierarchy protects you

- **Property Managers and Facility Admins** see organization-wide health: every facility, every vendor, every project, every compliance status.
- **Facility Managers** see only the facilities they're assigned to manage and their open projects. They can't see other facilities' vendors, costs, or compliance issues.

That structure means you can delegate safely — your on-site managers do their job without seeing data they shouldn't.

Add all your facilities under your organization. That gives you the cross-portfolio view that makes the rest worthwhile. Once that's done, [Portfolio Compliance](/property-managers/portfolio-compliance) becomes your daily 5-minute check-in.
