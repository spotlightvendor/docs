## About SpotlightVendor

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is SpotlightVendor?">
    SpotlightVendor is a procurement platform for senior living and healthcare facilities. It centralizes vendor compliance, lets you post project requests and get bids from your existing vendor network or discover new ones, and keeps an audit-ready trail along the way.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who uses SpotlightVendor?">
    Three audiences: senior care operators (single-facility and multi-site), the operations and compliance staff who run vendor processes, and the vendors who serve those communities.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is this different from managing vendors in a spreadsheet?">
    Vendors maintain their own profiles and documents, so you stop chasing paperwork. Expirations alert automatically 30 days out. RFPs route to matching vendors. Corporate teams get portfolio-wide visibility across every community. Everything is audit-ready by default.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Facilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I invite my existing vendors?">
    From your dashboard, send invitations directly. Vendors get an email, create their profile, upload their compliance documents, and join your network — no extra work for your team.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What compliance documents do you track?">
    W-9, COI, Workers' Comp, and any facility-specific documents you require. Each document has an expiration date with automatic 30-day alerts to the vendor.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I require different documents at different facilities?">
    Yes. Approve a vendor at the organization level for a baseline, then layer in stricter requirements at the facility level when needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Vendors

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I get found by facilities?">
    Build a verified profile with current compliance documents. Pro vendors are matched to RFPs in their service categories and visible to facilities searching the network.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if my insurance expires?">
    Your account drops to read-only. You can't bid on new work or accept invitations until you upload renewed documents. We email you 30 days before any document expires so you have time to renew.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
