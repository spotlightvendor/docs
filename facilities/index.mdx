## What we recommend doing first

1. **Add your existing vendors.** Send invitations from the Vendors tab. They get a free SpotlightVendor account and an automatic prompt to upload their COI, W-9, and workers' comp.
2. **Post one RFP** — even a simple one — to learn the bid flow before you actually need it for a real project.
3. **Bookmark the Compliance dashboard.** That's where every expiring document lives, and where you'll spend ~5 minutes per month catching issues before they become regulatory findings.
