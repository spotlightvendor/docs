## What you get

| | |
|---|---|
| **Audit-ready compliance** | W-9, COI, Workers' Comp tracked for every vendor with automatic 30-day expiration alerts. Pass surveys and insurance reviews without a spreadsheet scramble. |
| **Vetting flexibility** | Approve a vendor once for your whole org, or require additional facility-specific documents on top when a particular site needs it. |
| **RFPs and bids** | Post requests, get competitive bids back, award work in one click, and track the project to completion. |
| **In-app chat** | Project conversations between you and vendors stay tied to the project. No lost email threads. |
| **Purchasing power** | Network members access negotiated vendor pricing not available individually. |
| **Role-based access** | Org admins see everything. Facility managers only see facilities they're assigned to. Delegation is safe. |

## What we recommend doing first

1. **Add your existing vendors.** Send invitations from the Vendors tab. They join free and get an automatic prompt to upload their compliance docs.
2. **Post one RFP** — even a low-stakes one — to learn the bid flow before you actually need it for a real project.
3. **Bookmark the Compliance dashboard.** That's where every expiring document lives, and where you'll spend ~5 minutes a month catching issues before they become regulatory findings.
